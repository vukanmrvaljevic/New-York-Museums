About The Museum

The Museum of Modern Art celebrates creativity, openness, tolerance, and generosity. The museum aims to be inclusive places—both onsite and online—where diverse cultural, artistic, social, and political positions are welcome. They are committed to sharing the most thought-provoking modern and contemporary art, and hope you will join us in exploring the art, ideas, and issues of our time.
